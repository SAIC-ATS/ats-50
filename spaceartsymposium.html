Space Art Symposium

April 11, 2019 (Thursday) - 4:15-5:30PM

Sharp Building
LeRoy Neiman Center, 1st floor
37 S. Wabash Ave.

Register here

Panel Speakers

Moderated by Mark SubbaRao, Director of the Space Vis Lab at the Adler Planetarium

Annick Bureaud

Art Critic/Director of Leonardo OLATS

Eduardo Kac

Professor and Chair of Art & Technology Studies

Xin Liu

Arts Curator for the Space Exploration Initiative at MIT

Gravity conditions all forms and behaviors produced on Earth, including art.

What new artistic forms and experiences can be created if both creators and audiences/participants are free from this constraint?

Join Annick Bureaud (Director, Leonardo OLATS), Eduardo Kac (Professor and Chair of Art and Technology Studies, SAIC), and Xin Liu (Arts Curator for the Space Exploration Initiative, MIT) for a conversation on the implications of cultural production in the context of zero-gravity; moderated by Mark SubbaRao (astronomer and Director of the Space Visualization Laboratory at Adler Planetarium).

As part of the 2019 French-American Innovation Midwest, this event is co-organized with the Cultural Service at the Consulate General of France in Chicago.
